Intelligence brief

Context for the missions ahead.

Every deployment balances drag, stability, and thermal headroom. Use this briefing to understand the systems you can command before logging into the simulator.

Adaptive aero

Koenigsegg’s SmartCluster monitors yaw, pitch, and wind to reshape the body in milliseconds. Simulator scenarios factor in how much headroom you leave for crosswind correction.

Thermal intelligence

Hybrid deployments draw from both the battery buffer and combustion power. Aggressive strategies generate heat that can trigger torque limiting. Manage the thermal delta carefully.

Surface dynamics

Runways differ wildly: resurfaced concrete, high-grip polymer, or frozen tarmac. Tire selection in the simulator acknowledges grip deltas pulled from testing data.

Aero lab

Precision airflow decides success more than horsepower.

Koenigsegg’s aero program manipulates every molecule of air. Between adaptive dampers, triplex suspension, and deployable flaps, the cars respond in real time to your commands.

Jesko Absolut’s drag coefficient, achieved with extended rear close-outs and reduced trip surfaces.
