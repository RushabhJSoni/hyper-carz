The Koenigsegg Regera

The Koenigsegg Regera is designed as a luxury megacar alternative to Koenigsegg’s traditional lightweight, track-focused machines. It combines a powerful twin-turbo V8 with three electric motors and cutting-edge battery power via Koenigsegg Direct Drive. This revolutionary system removes the traditional gearbox, making the car lighter and more efficient while still delivering mind-bending power.

The Koenigsegg Jesko Absolut

The Jesko Absolut is the fastest Koenigsegg ever built and represents the brand’s final word in top-speed records for series-production road cars. Every body panel has been carefully sculpted to reduce drag and turbulent airflow while maintaining high-speed stability. Although just as potent as the track-focused Jesko, the Absolut is the stealthier, more streamlined interpretation designed purely for outright speed.

The Koenigsegg Gemera

The Gemera introduces four-seat practicality to the megacar world without sacrificing staggering performance. It blends extreme capability with a spacious interior and innovative hybrid technology, ensuring that mega-GT comfort and environmental responsibility can coexist with Koenigsegg-level thrills.
